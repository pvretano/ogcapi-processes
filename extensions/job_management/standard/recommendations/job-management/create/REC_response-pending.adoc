[[per_job-management_create_response-pending]]
[permission]
====
[%metadata]
identifier:: /per/job-management/create-response-pending

[.component,class=conditions]
--
. The link:https://docs.ogc.org/DRAFTS/18-062r3.html#sc_execution_mode[negotiated execution mode] indicated
  a `status="created"` preference at job submission.
. Other execution preference may or may not have been indicated in the request.
--

[.component,class=part]
--
The server SHOULD respond with `Preference-Applied: status="created"` asserting that the pending job submission
has been considered and will not start processing immediately.
--

[.component,class=part]
--
Other execution mode indicators SHOULD be omitted from `Preference-Applied` to avoid misinterpreting the response.
--

====
