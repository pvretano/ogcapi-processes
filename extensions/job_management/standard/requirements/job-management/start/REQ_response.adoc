[[req_job-management_start_response]]
[requirement]
====
[%metadata]
identifier:: /req/job-management/start-response

[.component,class=part]
--
A successful execution of the operation SHALL be reported as a response with a HTTP status code `200`.
--

[.component,class=part]
--
The job `status` SHALL be updated from `created` to `accepted` or `running` as deemed appropriate by the server
and accordingly to the predefined job parametrization.
--

[.component,class=part]
--
A response SHALL be a document that conforms to
https://raw.githubusercontent.com/opengeospatial/ogcapi-processes/master/openapi/schemas/processes-core/statusInfo.yaml[statusInfo.yaml].
--

====
